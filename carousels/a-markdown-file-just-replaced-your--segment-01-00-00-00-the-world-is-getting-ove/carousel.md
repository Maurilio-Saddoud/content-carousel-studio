eyebrow: DESIGN WORKFLOWS
# AI won’t replace designers.

It will replace bad handoffs.

---

eyebrow: THE REAL SHIFT
# Most teams are debating jobs.

They should be debating workflow.

The old product → design → engineering relay race is getting compressed.

---

eyebrow: WHY THIS MATTERS
# The expensive part was never just design.

It was waiting.

Waiting for mockups, waiting for feasibility, waiting to find out the idea broke in implementation.

---

eyebrow: WHAT CHANGES
# Now the design loop is closer to the build loop.

You can describe the app in English,
see the UI,
and move faster toward something buildable.

---

eyebrow: THE WIN
# AI deletes handoffs.

That means fewer translation errors,
less context loss,
and faster iteration across the whole team.

---

eyebrow: THE CATCH
# This does not make taste irrelevant.

It makes taste show up later,
where refinement, judgment, and polish actually matter.

---

eyebrow: OPERATOR TAKE
# The teams that win won’t be the ones with “AI design tools.”

They’ll be the ones that redesign the workflow around them.
