# Go try to buy from your competitors with AI.

Seriously.
Make that the exercise.

---

# Open Claude or ChatGPT and try to complete a transaction.

Not research.
Not summarize.

Actually try to buy.
Book.
Sign up.
Move money.
Finish the workflow.

---

# You will learn more from that test than from another strategy deck.

Because it forces you to see where agent-driven demand breaks in the real world.

---

# Most businesses are not ready for AI-mediated commerce.

The journey still assumes
human patience,
human clicking,
and human tolerance for friction.

That assumption is expiring.

---

# The point is not to talk about the future.

The point is to find out how close it already is.

And whether your business is ready when it shows up.
