eyebrow: FAILURE PATTERNS
# Most people think AI fluency means using the tool well.

It doesn’t.

---

eyebrow: THE REAL TEST
# Real AI fluency is diagnosing why the system failed.

Noticing the miss.
Finding the root cause.
Fixing the harness.

---

eyebrow: THIS IS WHY IT MATTERS
# Plausible wrong is more dangerous than obvious wrong.

Silent failures are expensive.
Because they pass casual review.

---

eyebrow: WHAT SERIOUS TEAMS LOOK FOR
# Serious teams track failure patterns, not vibes.

Tool choice errors.
Spec drift.
Context degradation.
Verification gaps.

That’s the actual work.

---

eyebrow: SIGNAL IN THE MARKET
# The market is already rewarding this skill.

When firms start certifying and scaling it,
that’s a clue:
this is becoming baseline operator literacy.

---

eyebrow: WHAT TO BUILD
# Don’t train teams to just prompt harder.

Teach them how to inspect,
verify,
and debug the whole system.

---

eyebrow: BOTTOM LINE
# The person who can explain
why the agent failed
is usually more valuable
than the person who made the demo look smooth.
