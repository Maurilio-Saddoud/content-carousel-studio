eyebrow: PRODUCT
# In product companies,
the shipped product is the value.

Everything else is support infrastructure.

---

eyebrow: CLARITY
# The roadmap is not the value.
The meeting is not the value.
The process is not the value.

The shipped thing is the value.

---

eyebrow: WHY THIS BREAKS MINDS
# A lot of organizations are built around
helping humans coordinate toward the artifact.

So when AI shortens idea → execution,
that support structure gets questioned.

---

eyebrow: WHAT CHANGES
# The closer people can get to the actual product,
the less tolerance there is for bloated translation layers.

That goes for product, design, engineering, and marketing.

---

eyebrow: TAKEAWAY
# If you want to understand AI’s impact,
start by asking:

What creates value?
And what merely helps humans coordinate around it?
