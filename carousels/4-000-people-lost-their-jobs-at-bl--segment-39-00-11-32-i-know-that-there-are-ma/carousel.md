# AI org change won’t happen all at once.

It will happen in ugly in-between states.

---

eyebrow: REALITY
# Most companies won’t jump straight
from old-school workflow to full agentic execution.

They’ll pass through messy middle layers.

---

eyebrow: WHAT THAT LOOKS LIKE
# Better briefs.
Smarter PRDs.
Faster iteration.
Less translation.

That still counts as progress.

---

eyebrow: THE MISTAKE
# Don’t confuse an intermediate gain
with the final form.

Saving time inside the old process is good.
But the bigger shift is redesigning the process itself.

---

eyebrow: TAKEAWAY
# Celebrate the in-between states.

Just don’t build your whole strategy
as if the in-between state is permanent.
