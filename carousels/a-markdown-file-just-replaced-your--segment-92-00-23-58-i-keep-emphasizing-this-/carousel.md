eyebrow: OPERATOR ADVANTAGE
# The floor dropped.

The ceiling didn’t.

---

eyebrow: WHAT PEOPLE GET WRONG
# When new creative tools arrive,
people panic about access.

But access was never the whole game.

---

eyebrow: WHAT ACTUALLY HAPPENS
# More people can produce good-enough work.

That lowers the floor.

It does not magically create excellent strategy,
taste,
or judgment.

---

eyebrow: THE NEW BOTTLENECK
# When generation gets cheap,
selection gets expensive.

Knowing what to make,
what to ship,
and what to ignore becomes the leverage.

---

eyebrow: WORKFLOW IMPLICATION
# The pipeline from idea to asset is becoming describable in English.

Runnable by agents.
Schedulable.
Repeatable.

---

eyebrow: SO WHAT NOW?
# If the tools are cheap,
then operator judgment matters more.

Bad taste scales faster too.

---

eyebrow: OPERATOR TAKE
# The edge is no longer “who can make something.”

It’s who can direct the system toward something worth making.
