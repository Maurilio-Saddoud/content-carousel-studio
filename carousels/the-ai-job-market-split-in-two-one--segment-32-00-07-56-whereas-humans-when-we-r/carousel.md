# AI is dangerous when it sounds correct.

It sounds right.

---

eyebrow: HUMAN VS AI
# Humans usually show tells when they’re lost.

They hesitate.
They contradict themselves.
They stumble.

---

eyebrow: THE AI VERSION
# AI can be fluently wrong.

Clean structure.
Confident tone.
Completely busted answer.

---

eyebrow: WHY THIS FOOLS TEAMS
# A polished output gets mistaken for a correct output.

That’s not intelligence.
That’s lazy review.

---

eyebrow: WHAT “TASTE” REALLY IS
# Good judgment here isn’t mystical taste.

It’s trained error detection.
You learn the failure patterns
and catch them fast.

---

eyebrow: PRACTICAL RULE
# Review AI output like it ships under your name.

If it would embarrass you in production,
it isn’t done.

---

eyebrow: OPERATOR ADVANTAGE
# The winners won’t be the people
who generate the most output.

They’ll be the ones
who can tell good from dangerous.
