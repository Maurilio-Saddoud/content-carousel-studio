eyebrow: ONBOARDING
# New hires are slow for one reason:

context transfer is brutally expensive.

---

eyebrow: NOT A TALENT PROBLEM
# A new engineer usually isn’t slow because they’re bad.

They’re slow because the company’s context is scattered everywhere.

Heads.
Docs.
Slack threads.
Old decisions.
Commit history.

---

eyebrow: THE REAL TAX
# For weeks, they’re reconstructing reality.

What matters.
Why decisions were made.
What not to break.
Who actually knows what.

That’s the tax.

---

eyebrow: WHY AI CHANGES THIS
# Systems that can ingest context faster
change the economics of onboarding.

The bottleneck stops being raw execution.
It becomes context design.

---

eyebrow: TAKEAWAY
# The advantage won’t just be better models.

It’ll be teams that package context cleanly enough
for humans and agents to ramp fast.
