eyebrow: MULTI-AGENT WORK
# The real multi-agent skill isn’t prompting.

It’s decomposition.

---

eyebrow: WHY TEAMS FREEZE
# A lot of people can use ChatGPT.

Then they hear “multi-agent system”
and suddenly treat it like black magic.

---

eyebrow: WHAT CHANGES
# Multi-agent work is closer to management than prompting.

Break the job into clear chunks.
Assign the right subtask.
Define the handoff.

---

eyebrow: WHERE PEOPLE MISS
# The bottleneck is not model access.

It’s whether you can separate
one messy project
into tractable pieces.

---

eyebrow: IMPORTANT CATCH
# Agents are not junior employees.

Humans can recover from vague direction.
Agents usually amplify it.

---

eyebrow: OPERATOR EDGE
# Good operators do 3 things well:

scope the task
spec the intent
review the output like it ships under their name

---

eyebrow: BOTTOM LINE
# Decompose and delegate well, and you’re ahead.

you’re already a lot closer
to running agent systems well
than most people think.
