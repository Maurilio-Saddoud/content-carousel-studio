# Enterprise AI usually fails
# where accountability gets fuzzy.

---

# That’s why humans still stay in the loop.

Not because enterprises are slow.

Because once AI touches important systems, somebody still needs to own the decision, the review, and the blast radius.

---

# This is where a lot of AI hype falls apart.

The demo says “fully autonomous.”

The real environment says:
- who approved this?
- who reviewed it?
- who is on the hook if it breaks?

---

# Enterprises are not just buying capability.

They are buying control.

That means auditability, review boundaries, escalation paths, and clear accountability.

Without that, the workflow does not survive contact with reality.

---

# The winning enterprise AI systems won’t remove humans everywhere.

They’ll put humans in the right places.

That is a much more serious design problem than most people realize.
