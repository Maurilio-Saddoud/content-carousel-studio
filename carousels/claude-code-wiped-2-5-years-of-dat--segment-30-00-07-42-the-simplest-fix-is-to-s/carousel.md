# The simplest AI reliability habit
is knowing when to start fresh.

---

eyebrow: MOST PEOPLE DON’T DO THIS
# They keep pushing the same session
long after it’s gone fuzzy.

Because restarting feels annoying.

---

eyebrow: BUT HERE’S THE TRADEOFF
# A restart costs minutes.

A degraded session can cost hours,
bad changes,
or quiet damage you notice later.

---

eyebrow: THE REFRAME
# Starting fresh is not admitting defeat.

It’s basic operational hygiene
when you work with agents.

---

eyebrow: TAKEAWAY
# If the context is drifting,
reset early.

That’s usually cheaper than “one more prompt.”
