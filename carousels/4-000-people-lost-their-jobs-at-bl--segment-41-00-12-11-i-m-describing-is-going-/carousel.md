# The transition period is the ugliest part.

That is the phase most people are reacting to right now.

---

# We are in the awkward middle.

Old workflows still exist.
New tools are clearly better.
And most teams are trying to bolt one onto the other.

That always looks messy.

---

# Of course people hate it.

Do you love spending 11.6 hours a week in meetings?

Do you love handoffs,
check-ins,
and process layers that keep multiplying?

Probably not.

---

# But the mess is not the destination.

It is what happens when the old org design has not caught up to the new capability.

The pain is real.
The current setup is temporary.

---

# The winners will redesign around the new reality faster.

Not just add AI on top of the old machine.

Actually change the shape of the work.
